How to use:
  • Hold Ctrl (or Cmd on Mac) and click a symbol, or press F12
  • The TypeScript pane is this library navigating its own source. Only src/index.ts is open — Ctrl+Click any export (DefinitionProvider, TsWorkerStrategy, WorkspaceIndex, …) and the real file is fetched on demand (watch the log), landing on the exact symbol. Keep jumping deeper from there.
  • Scope-aware Python: the value inside scaled() jumps to the local, not the module global; helper and CONSTANT lazy-load utils.py
  • Clicking inside strings or comments does nothing — it's a real parser, not regex
